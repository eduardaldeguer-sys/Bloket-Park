Family Zone

The Family Zone is designed for visitors of all ages. It includes playgrounds, picnic areas, interactive games, and safe entertainment spaces for children and parents. This area encourages bonding, fun activities, and shared experiences.

Action Zone

The Action Zone is full of excitement and energy. Visitors can enjoy sports courts, climbing walls, adventure courses, and thrilling attractions. It is perfect for those who love adrenaline and physical challenges.

Quiet Zone

The Quiet Zone offers a peaceful and relaxing environment. It includes reading areas, meditation spaces, shaded seating, and calm walking paths. This zone is ideal for visitors who want to relax and recharge.

Green Zone

The Green Zone celebrates nature and sustainability. It features gardens, eco-friendly spaces, tree areas, and environmental education activities. Visitors can connect with nature and learn about protecting the environment.
